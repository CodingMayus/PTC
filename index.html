SEA DEBRIS OCEAN PROMOTION

Watch where your trash ends up!

The Problem.

Welcome to our project on ocean protection, focusing on the arguably the biggest problem facing the Oceans: Trash. Islands of them results in the destruction of thousands of kilometers of ocean. But what might be argubly the most unexpected fact: Most trash gets lost in the sea.

Our Solution.

Tackle the problem directly by simulating real-ocean current environments using reliable API data- enabling us to see where trash will end up.
Place trash in the map for it to simulate where the trash will end up. 2 months into the future? 5? A year? It's your choice.
